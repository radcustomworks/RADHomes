About RAD Custom Works

RAD Custom Works is created with the passion to develop IoT products that offer solutions which are not just smart but intuitive and multi-faceted. In the current eco-system, there are several apps and cloud-based services that make homes ‘smart(er)’ in a fragmented manner. However, such an eco-system does not create a seamless smart home experience. RAD Custom Works brings together home automation solutions and products from different brands in a harmonious fashion, one where a home is truly smart and integrated with various technologies that are enabling the process.

We believe that the best way to ensure transition to a new technology is by leveraging existing products/technology in a space and ensuring least possible disruption in the course of the transition to a new technology.

A ‘smart home’ is more than just smart devices working on an app or through a voice assistant. Our endeavour is to create a unique and seamless experience while being mindful of the constraints and challenges of the physical environment and requirements of every user in a home.
